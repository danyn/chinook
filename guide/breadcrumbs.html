# BREADCRUMBS

Breadcrumbs are used by both your customers and google. You can set your breadcrumbs to follow categories that you set up, so that no matter how a user lands on your product page they see the right categories.

Chinook allows you to set up a navigaion menu to handle this. A pair of woman's shoes could read as follows:

Example Breadcrumb

womens > apperal > shoes

You can also enable deeper four level breadcrumbs:

Example Breadcrumb

womens > fall fashion > apperal > shoes
